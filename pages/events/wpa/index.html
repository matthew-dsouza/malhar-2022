Multiverse of Madness

For this event we welcome participants to perform a dynamic duet on pre-existing songs rewritten by them. For the eliminations participants must rewrite the songs in a different POV, while the Finals will require them to change the narrative of the song with the re-written lyrics.

No. of participants: 2 per team

Open to: Contingents and PRNC (Not outstation)

Time Duration:
3 hours
Performance time: 6-8 minutes

Location: College Hall

Starrin' the streets

Teams will be assigned a decade of popular music randomly (ex. 1970s - Disco, etc.). Participants are required pay homage to any iconic musician or dancer from the era for the finals. The dance styles are restricted to street dance styles like Hip hop, waacking, Freestyle and so on.

No. of participants: 5-7 per team

Open to: Contingents and PRNC (Not outstation)

Time Duration:
3 hours
Setup time : 1 minute
Performance time : 5-6 minutes

Location: Quad
