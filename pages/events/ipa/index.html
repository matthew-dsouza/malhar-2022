Malang

Teams will be provided with a social issue and an Indian art form which they have to incorporate into their plays.

No. of participants: 10-12 per team

Open to: Contingents and PRNC (Not outstation)

Time Duration:
3.5 hours
Performance time: 12-15 minutes per team

Location: Quad

‘Folk’-us on your Element

Participants have to choose any one of the 5 following elements (Earth, Fire, Water, Wind, Aether) and perform on music that is regional and authentic. The choreography must portray the chosen element.

No. of participants: 8-12 per team

Open to: Contingents and PRNC's (Not outstation)

Time Duration:
3 hours
3-5 minutes per team
Performance Time: 30 seconds

Location: Foyer

Arohan

Participants will be required to either sing a song in Hindi or Marathi or in a combination of both with the accompaniment of instruments.

No. of participants: 2-3 per team

Open to: Contingents and PRNC (Not outstation)

Time Duration:
3 hours
Set Up Time: 2 minutes per team
Performance Time: 4 minutes per team

Location: MMR

The teams will be given an iconic bollywood movie which they will have to depict through freestyle dancing incorporating a solo performance in addition to using props.

No. of participants: 6-8 per team

Open to: Contingents and PRNC (Not outstation)

Time Duration:
3 hours

Taal se taal mila

Participants have to perform pure classical dance or pure classical music. They can experiment with the costumes and the props. They need to include a “x” factor which would set them apart from other dances.

No. of participants: 2-8 per team

Open to: Contingents and PRNC (Not outstation)

Time Duration:
2 hours
Set up time: 45 secs
Performance time: 3-4 minutes
